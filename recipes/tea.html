It's always tea time!

Tea is an aromatic, refreshening beverage widely consumed across the Indian subcontinent. Tea is the go-to beverage for indians as it is quick and easy to make, available everywhere and has been part of the indian culture for a long time. It is usually consumed hot along with snacks like pakoras, namkeen, biscuits etc.

Humble ingredients for a humble cup of tea.

  • Water
  • Tea Granules
  • Sugar
  • Ginger
  • Milk
  • Cardamom

Steps to follow:

  1. Decide how much tea you want to prepare.
  2. Take half as much water i.e. 50% of the total volume(+compensation for boiling say 0.1x water).
  3. Add little nore than 1/2 regular tablespoon of tea granules per regular tea cup.
  4. Add 1 regular tablespoon of sugar per regular tea cup.
  5. Crush ginger in an utensil and then add crushed ginger to boiling mixture( some ginger juice gets left behind in the utensil so you can pour some mix into the utensil and back to the boiling mix), quantity of ginger will get optimized as you get more experience.
  6. Crush cardamom as per your need and add it to the mix and let the tea boil for a good while.
  7. Sit back and have some tea and tell me was it a.
